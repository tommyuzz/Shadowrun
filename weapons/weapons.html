Archive scope

Core Rulebook melee weapons, projectile weapons, firearms, heavy weapons, grenades, rockets, and missiles. Ammunition and accessories are indexed separately.

Weapon field key

ACC
Accuracy is the weapon's inherent limit for attack tests, before applicable modifications.
DV
Damage Value is the weapon's base damage before net hits and other modifiers.
AP
Armor Penetration modifies the Armor rating used for the Damage Resistance Test.
RC
Recoil Compensation offsets recoil from firing multiple rounds. A value in parentheses is conditional.
MODE
Firing Mode: SS is Single-Shot, SA is Semi-Automatic, BF is Burst Fire, and FA is Full Auto.
